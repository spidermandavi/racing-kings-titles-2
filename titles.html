Titles

RKWC

Racing Kings World Champion

  • Have won at least one RKWC (Racing Kings World Championship)

RKSGM

Racing Kings Super Grandmaster

  • 5000 or more rated RK games
  • Have reached 2500 rating
  • 2 rated RK tournament wins with at least 10 games and a performance of at least 2400
  • 5 rated RK tournament performances of at least 2400 with a minimum of 10 games
  • 1 RK Shield win

RKGM

Racing Kings Grandmaster

  • 3000 or more rated RK games
  • Have reached 2400 rating
  • 1 rated RK tournament win with at least 10 games and a performance of at least 2400
  • 3 rated RK tournament performances of at least 2400 with a minimum of 10 games

RKIM

Racing Kings International Master

  • 2000 or more rated RK games
  • Have reached 2300 rating
  • 3 rated RK tournament wins with at least 10 games and performances of at least 2300

RKM

Racing Kings Master

  • 1000 or more rated RK games
  • Have reached 2200 rating
  • 3 rated RK tournament wins with at least 10 games and performances of at least 2200

RKCM

Racing Kings Candidate Master

  • 500 or more rated RK games
  • Have reached 2100 rating
  • 3 rated RK tournament wins with at least 10 games and performances of at least 2100

RKV

Racing Kings Veteran

  • 10000 or more rated RK games
  • Or have been playing RK for at least 5 years

RKHM

Racing Kings Honourary Master

  • Have accomplished something great for the RK community (must be recognized)
